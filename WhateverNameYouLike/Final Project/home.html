What Inspires Us to Learn?

We are inspired to learn through curiousity and the discovery of new ideas. The need within us to know about the world around us and how the puzzle pieces of life fit together leads us to ascertain who we are both within ourselves and in our lives. The feeling of success when we learn everything there is about a topic or overcome a challenge creates such a feeling of elation that we feel as though we can take on the world if even for a little bit. Learning is a way for us to ground ourselves to the solid in a world of constant, shifting change that can be difficult to navigate. All of these are our inspiration to learn.

How Does Learning Impact Us?

We are impacted by learning from the opportunities that are afforded us. The knowledge that comes from learning allows us to make decisions without an overpowering feeling of unsurety and anxiety. We gain confidence within ourselves and feel more courageous, stepping out and seizing the moment before us. The exploration of knowledge also allows us to learn more about ourselves: our strengths, our weaknesses, and what makes us unique. The impact that learning has on us allows us to become better and adapt, creating the world we want and causing the change that needs to happen in the world.
