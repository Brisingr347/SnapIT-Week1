Graphic Design

One of the most important factors of any website or application is how visually appealing it is to the viewers. The Graphic Design industry has become a crucial part of the IT industry, both growing with demand. As technology keeps growing and expanding, competition is growing between companies. This is creating more demand in the Graphic Design and creating more job opportunities for those who are interested.

My Motivation

My favorite part of this internship has been getting to design how the elements look on a webpage. The placement, the color palettes, and what images look best with the decided upon visuals have all been a delight for me. I've enjoyed finding color palettes for years and have contemplated joining the Graphic Design industry multiple times in the past. Knowing now that it ties into the IT industry adds another draw to the idea.

How SnapIT has Motivated Me

SnapIT started out as a motivation to learn code in the first place which has only grown as I've learned throughout the past 7 weeks. In addition, SnapIT has encouraged me to explore the world of coding and the designs it can create. From different Unicodes, dozens of ways to code to get the same result, and the fun add-ons that can be added onto any page, I've gotten to explore and see them because of SnapIT. I hope that I can learn more and use my experience with SnapIT as a stepping stone for the future.

My Next Experience

I plan to learn more about making a website or application more visually appealing. This includes animation, transitions, and color manipulation. I'm also going to be helping my dad with several HTML databases and learning how to manage the programming for those and the results they create.
